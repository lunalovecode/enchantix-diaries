Wensday Wednesday, November 14

The project went something like this:

Me: On the right, we have Soleil Starborne. A rare hybrid of the "ace student" and "extrovert" species.

Soleil: Oh, hi!

Blackthorne: Extremely eager to help others to a concerning degree. May or may not turn out to be a workaholic in ten years' time.

Soleil: Okay...?

Me: On the left, we have Blossom Valentine.

Blossom: Hi!

Me: Sweet and energetic.

Blackthorne: We are beginning to suspect that she is not a fairy, but a bag of sugar disguised as one.

Blossom: Um... Thanks...?

Me: Now we have Heath Arborea.

Heath: Hi. [Blackthorne creates a vine at her feet. When Heath investigates, she is pulled down by it.] Woah!

Blackthorne: Rather gull—

Me: —hard to provoke. But if you somehow manage to, RUN. Her plant magic is unmatched by anyone at this school.

Heath: Aww...

Me: And the only one who can one-up her in terms of physical strength is Ari. Who so happens to be her older brother. No one has crossed her unscathed. [Blackthorne glares at me.] I'm just saying it as it is. Have you fought her?

Blackthorne: Stay quiet. In front of us are Khione Frostfall, Ariadne Hallows, and Persephone Tempest, otherwise known as the "New Trix." They are rarely seen apart.

Me: They bully other students for no reason other than their own amusement. When you attack one of them, all three of them can and WILL beat you to a pulp and make it look like an accident.

Blackthorne: And now we have Hyun-mi Seo. A lose—

Me: —lonely—

Blackthorne: —specimen that doesn't have frie—

Me: —prefers to spend her time alone.
